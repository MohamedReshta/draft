face-recognition

this model classify students into recognised educational patterns The principles were used to classify students, and this was done by photographing them using cameras designated for that purpose and classifying each student individually based on the generally accepted principles.

sign-session

through which reactions are taken from students after the lecture in real time, by photographing them using the cameras designated for that. This allows those responsible for delivering the information to know the extent of the student’s comprehension of the scientific material.
